About NCC & NSS

NCC and NSS help students develop discipline, leadership, confidence, and social responsibility. We prepare the youth of today to tackle the challenges of tomorrow.

NCC SECTION

Why Join NCC

Build unshakeable leadership, absolute confidence, and military-grade discipline.

Benefits

Earn 'B' & 'C' certificates, get defense career preferences, and personal grooming.

Activities

National camps, institutional firing practices, ceremonial parades, and adventurous trekking.

NSS SECTION

Social Service

Massive plantation drives, rural literacy awareness, and community development work.

Activities

Annual blood donation camps, disaster relief management, and cleanliness drives under Swachh Bharat.

Benefits

Develop empathetic teamwork, dynamic communication skills, and democratic leadership.
